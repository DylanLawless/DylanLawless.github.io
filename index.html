This website is under live development. Eventually, it will house pages on sevral topics that are relevant to human precision medicine and genomics. This includes technical examples of statistical methods, best practices, general reading, and blog posts on a range of topics. One goal is to fully explain details of research methods that are used for each topic. Within the year 2020, the major topics will be outlined and populated with a lot of prewritten articles and pages. Some placeholder pages are included for testing. In the mean time, the formatting may be ugly, the content incomplete, and working notes may be rough.
